How To Claim A Club Record

To claim a record, the following criteria must be fulfilled:

  • The score must be shot in competition conditions at SSS, BUCS, BUTC, or Record Status competitions.
  • The claimant must have partook in the competition representing Napier University Archery Club.
  • The claim must be accompanied by the official competition results released by the organiser.
To claim a record, simply email your claim to to the club with the supporting competition results.
